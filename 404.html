a quiet place between stars

watch your step, traveller...

an automated message plays, making you jump.

“Caution: You have reached the edge of containment.”

the bulkhead you opened leads nowhere — or perhaps, it once did.

the hum of the station doesn’t reach here.

no beacons. no guidance. just silence, with the occasional soft, eerie creak of old metal shifting in vacuum.

do not remain here too long. the silence starts to echo back.


return while you still remember the way.
